FAQ

Common questions

Here are some of the most common questions that we get.
How can I book a private appointment with the makeup artist? You can easily book a private appointment by visiting the 'Book Appointment' section on the website and selecting your preferred date and time.
What makeup services are offered for private appointments? Private appointments include personalized makeup sessions for special events, bridal makeup, editorial looks, and more. The makeup artist will work with you to create the perfect look based on your preferences.
Are makeup classes available for beginners? Yes, makeup classes cater to all skill levels, including beginners. The makeup artist will provide step-by-step guidance and tips to help you enhance your makeup skills.
How do I pay for my appointment or class? Payment for appointments and classes can be made securely through the website using major credit cards or other accepted payment methods. Payment details will be provided during the booking process.
Can I reschedule or cancel my appointment? Yes, you can reschedule or cancel your appointment by contacting the makeup artist directly at least 24 hours in advance. Please refer to the booking confirmation email for more details on rescheduling or cancelation policies.
